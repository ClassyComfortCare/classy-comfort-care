Welcome to Classy Comfort Care

Classy Comfort Care is an approved MDHHS Home Help Agency provider proudly serving Oakland County and Wayne County in Michigan. We are dedicated to providing compassionate, reliable home care services that promote comfort, dignity, and independence.

About Us

With over 10 years of experience, Classy Comfort Care offers professional home help services tailored to each individual's needs. Our caregivers are fully trained and certified to provide exceptional care and support to our clients in the comfort of their own homes.
